About Jovian

Jovian is a platform for sharing and collaborating on Jupyter notebooks. We are a team of data scientists, engineers, and designers who are passionate about making data science accessible to everyone. We believe that Jupyter notebooks are the future of data science and we are building tools to make it easier to work with them.
